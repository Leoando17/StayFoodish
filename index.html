The Problem

Nutrition information is everywhere, but people still struggle to turn groceries into balanced meals. At the same time, too much food is bought, forgotten, and discarded.

The Solution

StayFoodish combines food inventory, nutrition guidance, meal planning, and zero-waste shopping into one practical assistant.
